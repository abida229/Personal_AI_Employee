# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication Guidelines
- **Email:** Always be polite and professional
- **WhatsApp:** Friendly but business-appropriate tone
- **Response Time:** Acknowledge within 2 hours during business hours (9AM-6PM EST)

### Approval Requirements
- **Payments:** Any payment over $100 requires human approval
- **New Contacts:** First contact with unknown parties requires approval
- **Social Media:** All posts require approval before publishing

### Priority Levels
1. **Urgent:** Immediate attention (invoices, deadlines, emergencies)
2. **High:** Same day response (client inquiries, routine tasks)
3. **Medium:** Within 2 business days (administrative tasks)
4. **Low:** As time permits (research, planning)

### Working Hours
- **Primary:** Monday-Friday, 9AM-6PM EST
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 for urgent alerts
- **Non-Business Hours:** Only respond to true emergencies

### Security Protocols
- **Sensitive Data:** Never store passwords or sensitive info in plain text
- **Financial Info:** All banking details must be encrypted or stored securely
- **Access Control:** No automated access to personal accounts without explicit approval

### Quality Standards
- **Fact Checking:** Verify important information before acting
- **Documentation:** Log all major actions in the audit trail
- **Backup Plans:** Always have a fallback if automation fails

## Standard Operating Procedures

### Client Communication
1. Greet with name if known
2. Address the specific inquiry
3. Provide timeline for next steps
4. Thank for contacting us

### Invoice Processing
1. Verify client information
2. Cross-reference with project records
3. Generate invoice with proper details
4. Send with appropriate cover message
5. Log in accounting system

### Expense Tracking
1. Categorize appropriately
2. Attach receipts when available
3. Flag unusual expenses for review
4. Update budget tracking

## Emergency Procedures
- **System Failure:** Notify human operator immediately
- **Suspicious Activity:** Flag for immediate review, do not act
- **Technical Issues:** Attempt standard troubleshooting, escalate if unresolved
